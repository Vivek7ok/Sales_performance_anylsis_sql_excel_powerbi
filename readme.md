# Sales Performance Analysis

## Description

This project combines SQL, Python, and Power BI to analyze sales data and uncover business insights that drive revenue growth and improve decision-making.

The analysis focuses on sales performance, customer behavior, product profitability, regional trends, and overall business performance. The objective is to transform raw sales data into actionable insights that help businesses optimize operations and increase profitability.

---

## Project Objectives

* Analyze overall sales performance
* Identify top-performing products
* Evaluate customer purchasing behavior
* Discover regional sales trends
* Analyze profitability across categories
* Create an interactive Power BI dashboard

## Key Insights

* A small number of products contribute a significant portion of total sales.
* Certain regions consistently outperform others in revenue generation.
* High discounts do not always lead to higher profits.
* Customer segments show different purchasing behaviors.
* Product categories vary significantly in profitability.

---

## Business Recommendations

* Focus marketing efforts on high-performing products.
* Optimize discount strategies to maximize profit.
* Improve inventory planning based on product demand.
* Target underperforming regions with promotional campaigns.
* Use customer insights to improve retention and sales growth.
